Gluten-free Chocolate Banana Bread

This gluten-free chocolate banana bread is a delicious way to use up a couple of overripe bananas!

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 minutes 55 minutes 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, room temp
  2. 3/4 cup sugar
  3. 2 eggs plus 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. 2 fresh bananas, mashed
  5. 1/2 cup sour cream
  6. 1 1/4 cup sweet rice flour or gluten-free flour blend
  7. 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  8. 1 tsp baking soda
  9. 1/2 tsp salt
  10. 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Prepare a loaf pan by spraying the bottom and sides with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, and beat until combined.
  4. Mix in mashed bananas and sour cream.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together gluten-free flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Sprinkle additional chocolate chips on top if desired.
  9. Bake for 35-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Cool completely before cutting and serving.

Notes:

  1. If your gluten-free flour blend already contains xanthan gum, you can omit it from the recipe.
  2. You can use regular baking cocoa if you don't have dark cocoa.
  3. If your loaf pan is too small, divide the batter between two pans and adjust baking time accordingly.
  4. You can use Greek yogurt instead of sour cream if preferred.
